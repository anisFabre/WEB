Dragon quest is a game I discovered when I was younger.

It was love at first sight knowing that it is the very famous akira toriyama who is responsible for the design of the characters.

It's a game that really reflects on certain aspects of life. That's why I strongly recommend this opus!

To sum up this game (I put links to pages if you are interested in the story), We start with our "birth" in the game and then wake up in a boat with a kind of 4-year-old elliopes.

At this point in history it is not known whether the hero's father is actually a king or if it is just a hallucination.

Indeed, by talking to the various sailors populating the boat we learn that our "father" is a famous mercenary.

You should know that in this game we mainly have 3 phases:

-the first: we're a child.

-the second: we are adults and we have the choice between 3 women (Pandora, Bianca and Nera) and then we go to our kingdom to govern it.

-the third: We have been frozen for 10 years and the two children of the hero are now able to accompany him in his quest.

The team that we have in the game

In combat you can only fight at 4 even if in total we are 8. You can replace one of your characters by another one that is in the chariot.

Some characters in the game

Hero

Hero of the game

He did not have an easy childhood and yet despite having been a slave of 4 years until 15-16 years he never abandoned the quest to find his mother

Nolan

Son of the hero & savior of the human kind

Nolan is the son of the hero, he is the only one who can use the sword of the legend. Despite his young age he will be very useful in the adventure.

Bianca

Wife of the hero & descending from zeniths

Bianca is the wife of the hero as well as the mother of Nolan and Inga. She is the hero’s childhood friend and both made the 400 hits during their childhood. The episode that brought both of them together as children was when they fought ghosts that haunted a castle.

Inga

Daughter of the Hero & Bianca

Inga is the daughter of the hero and Bianca. She was born at the same time as her brother, so they are twins. However it was her brother who received the blessing of the sword. As for her, she inherited the magical powers of her mother

Smilo

A smilodon

Smilo is a monster of the smilodon race. He was saved when he was still small by bianca and the hero when they were also children. From that day on they became friends.
